About Us

The Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) is a key agency in the Philippines dedicated to ensuring public safety through fire prevention, suppression, and investigation. Established under Republic Act 6975, the BFP operates under the Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG) and plays a crucial role in protecting lives and properties from fire and other emergencies.


Mission

To prevent and suppress destructive fires, investigate their causes, enforce fire-related laws, and promote fire safety awareness and education among the public.


Vision

A modern fire service fully capable of ensuring a fire-safe naton by 2034.
